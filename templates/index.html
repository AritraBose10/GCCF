📐 How ranking works
1. Accuracy first — more GCCF courses completed = higher rank, always.
2. Speed tiebreaker — if tied on courses, whoever finished their last course earlier wins.
Example: A finishes 8 courses in 10 days · B finishes only 7 in 2 days → A wins. But if B also finishes 8, 1 day earlier → B wins.
